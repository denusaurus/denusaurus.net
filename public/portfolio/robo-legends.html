Robo Legends
Concept art for an ongoing school project "Robo Legends". It's a developing video game that serves as an exercise for volunteer students who can create and animate their own character for a fighting game. The game takes place in an abandoned scrapyard and the characters are robots made of scraps with unique fighting abilites. My character, Gadjet, is an old repurposed Macintosh computer with an android body and pistons for arms that carries her computer as a backpack and can hack into other computer systems with the help of her keyboard.
