Fokoro
Character animation for an ongoing school project "Fokoro". It's an open-ended video game collaboration between university lecturer Javad Khajavi and his students, that serves as an exercise for volunteer students, who can contribute to the video game concept in any discipline they wish to explore. The game is a 2D action-adventure platformer, inspired by Japanese culture. aesthetics and folkore and is set in a forest full of mythical creatures that help or hinder the main character, who can shapeshift into an owl. For my own contribution, I wished to explore the character animation for this type of video game and provided the first concepts for the various stages of the main characters movement patterns (idle, run, jump, sit, attacks).
