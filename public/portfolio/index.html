Die Schule der magischen Tiere
Illustrations for the German children's book series "Die Schule der magischen Tiere", written by Margit Auer and published by Carlsen. The team behind the redesign is called "die Farbfüchse" and consists of Celia Kaspar, the team leader and character designer, Lucy-Sophie Witthoff and me. I joined the team in February of 2025 and the project is ongoing as of today.
Books 1-4 & 16
I started by only doing the lineart on small vignettes and gradually moved to more complex and fully rendered illustrations.
Books 5-10 & "Eingeschneit"
I started taking on more responsibilities. I was now in charge of the fully colored double-page spread on the pastedown, in addition to some illustrations in the text.

Tender is the Knight

Tender is the Knight
"Tender is the Knight" is the title of my Bachelor film, directed and produced by Martine Samdal and me, during our final year at Volda University College. It's a short film about the lonely knight Angus, looking for love on the dating app "Tender" and it's available on YouTube:)
We worked with Procreate for the pre-production, animated in Toon Boom Harmony and composited in Adobe After Effects.

About me

About me
Hi! I'm Dani (she/her).
I'm a 25 year-old freelancing artist based in Bratislava, Slovakia.
I have a Bachelor in Animation from Volda University College in Norway and I've worked on different animated short films and TV series and I'm currently illustrating a children's book series.
Always nagged by teachers for doodling and not paying attention, my passion for art turned serious when I chose an art high school and extracurricular drawing classes where I could flourish. I briefly studied art history ate the University of Vienna, Austria, but interrupted my boring academic studies to regain my passion by pursuing animation in my hometown. After a very convincing Erasmus semester, I finished my degree in Norway.
In my spare time I enjoy working on my own creative projects, tinkering on my website, playing videogames and going on adventures with my dog. I also speak eight languages and I'm learning FIVE more (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Japanese and Dog).
