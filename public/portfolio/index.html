About me

About me

Hi! I'm Dani (she/her).
I'm a 25 year-old freelancing artist based in Bratislava, Slovakia.

I have a Bachelor in Animation from Høgskulen i Volda, Norway and I've been working on different animated short films and TV series, as well as illustrating a children's book series.

I grew up in Bratislava, but I moved to Luxembourg when I was 13, where I attended an art high school and extracurricular drawing classes. I briefly studied art history in Vienna, Austria, but interrupted my studies to pursue animation. I got a Bachelor's degree from Volda University College in Norway.

I've since worked on a couple animated short films and a children's TV show, and I'm currently illustrating a children's book series.

In my spare time I enjoy working on my own creative projects, watching movies or long YouTube essays, and hitting the gym or going for a run. I also speak eight languages and I'm learning three more (HTML, CSS and JavaScript).
